Speed Racer

A Classic 2d Racing Game programmed by Collin Maryniak

With help from Cyrus Hoover and Chaos Defender

Programmed using Python

How to Play

Download the game first with the above button!

Use the up and down arrow keys or the W and S keys to move up
or down a lane. Avoid the obstacles such as barrels, oil puddles,
and rocks. Your score increases by 10 every time you pass an
obstacle. Try and get a new personal best and beat your friends!
